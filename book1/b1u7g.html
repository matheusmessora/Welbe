1. PLANNED FUTURE


The planned future is used to speak about something that will happen in the future and the rule is:


SUBJECT + TO BE + GOING TO + MAIN VERB + COMPLEMENT

Examples: He is going to play soccer on Saturday.
Are you going to travel next holiday?
I'm not going to study next week.
NOTICE: What are you gonna do next Saturday night?

2. THE TIME


The time in English is divided in AM (Anti Meridian) or PM (Post Meridian). The common question used to know the time is: What time is it?

7:00 = It's seven o'clock. = It's seven in sharp.
7:15 = It's seven fifteen. = It's fifteen past seven. = It's a quarter after seven.
7:30 = It's seven thirty. = It's thirty after seven. = It's half past seven.
7:45 = It's seven forty-five. = It's fifteen to eight. = It's a quarter before eight.

3. DIRECTIONS


Turn right or turn left / go ahead (on) / on the corner and at the corner.

Example: Where is the supermarket?
Weel, you are at the corner of Paulista Avenue and Bela Cintra Street, so go ahead on Paulista Avenue and after six blocks, on the corner, you turn left, walk two more blocks and turn right.
