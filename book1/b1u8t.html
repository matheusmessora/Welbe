Habits

Do you know what habits are? Well, they are things that people commonly do. For example, I always sleep less than six hours per night. It is a very bad habit, because according to experts, the correct is to sleep eight hours per night. I usually listen to music during the day. It normally happens when I drive my car. Sometimes I watched the news on TV and sometimes I just read the news on internet. For me, it is essential to know what is going on in the world.
I like to play with my kids. Unfortunately, I seldom do it because my time is always short, but when I am free at home, we often have fun together. The kids are always ready to have fun.

I rarely go to the beach now, because of my job. However, when I go I always take my surfboard with me. I love this sport. I usually listen to surfers saying that surf isn't a sport, it is a life style. Anyway, it is a good way to keep in shape.
I never watch reality shows on TV, because I really think they are very empty and as my time is short, I don't spend it on silly things like this.
Well, now that you know my habits, tell me about yours. How often do you go to the cinema? How often do you practice sports? How often do you travel to have fun? How often do you do things that make you happy?
Can you see? If you answer these questions above, you are just speaking about your habits. I hope you have good habits. If not, there is timie to change them.
