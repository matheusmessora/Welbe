A day in the park
It is a very beautiful and warm day. There are no clouds in the sky. It is a perfect day to go to the park and it is exactly what the Stewarts do.
The Stewart family is big. The father, Mr. Stewart, is now playing with his sons, John and Mike. They are playing soccer with their new ball.
Mrs. Stewart, the mother, is preparing a picnic with her daughter Jane. They are putting the fruits on a big table cloth.
They are different kinds of fruits.
They have apples, guaves, pearls, bananas, grapes, peaches, plums and oranges, too.
The Stewarts know how important to eat fruit is.
They have other things, too. For example, carrot cakes, chocolate cookies and some sliced bread with strawberry jam, they love strawberry jam.

To drink, there are three different things, mango juice, which is Mr.

Stewart's favorite one, papaya juice for the boys and pineapple juice for Mrs. Stewart and Jane. And of course, much water.

When everything is ready to the picnic, Mrs. Stewart calls her husband and her kids to ear, but before, they need to wash their hands.

There are many things to do in the park in a sunny day. So, next time you have a beautiful day and it is your day off, go to the park to have fun.
