A JOB INTERVIEW
Susan is a twenty-year-old marketing student who is unemployed. Her parents don't have lots of condition to afford the house and her studies, so she needs to get a job quickly. Today, she has an interview at Welbe Corporation. It is a company which works in the field of education and wants to set up a new school in south of Brazil. The company need someone to help the marketing manager. Susan is a little nervous, but I think it is common in a job interview. While she is waiting for the interviewer, she talks to the secretary. - It is a beautiful day, isn't it? - Yes, I really like sunny days. - So do I. I feel lively to do everything. -So, you are here for the marketing assistant vacancy, right? - Yes, I really need this job. My parents do not have enough money to afford the house and my studies and I wouldn't like to stop the university. - Do you have experience in the marketing area?

- To tell you the truth, I don't have, but I am really willingwilling to work in the area and have no difficulties to learn a new task. I like so much to face new challenges. - So, maybe you could tell the manager that you have experience and I think you can get the job. - I don't think it's a good idea. I can't lie. It is not fair. - Ok, by the way, your name is Susan, right? Nice to meet tou Susan, I am Monica, your new boss. Congratulations, the job is yours.
