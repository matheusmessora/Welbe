The exchange student

After a couple of years studying English, you finally decide to face the challenge of travelling to a country where English is the first language. It appears to be simply great and it is, but you have got to take lots of decisions before going.
The first decision is to choose the country. There are many possibilities; however you need to take just one. To make it easier, you should think about the pros and cons each country has. Consider the weather to make your choice easier. Some countries such as the USA and Canada seem to be perfect because of the distance from Brazil and the time zone, however from November to April, depending on the city you choose, you will freeze. So, think carefully when you are going.

Australia and New Zealand are simply wonderful, though the time zone may be one problem to some people; eleven and thirteen hours forward respectively. It seems to be nothing, but when you travel to one of these countries, your sensation is like two days travelling. South Africa is a paradise. It has beautiful and exotic beaches and places.
