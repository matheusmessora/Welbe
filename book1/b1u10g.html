GRAMMAR - CROOKS

1.WHY AND BECAUSE

Why is an interrogative pronoun and is more frequently used in questions.
Because is a conjunction and is used in answers.


Example:
Why do study English? I study because it is very important.
Because can be abbreviated like: 'cos / 'cause (informal)


2. ADJECTIVES

The adjectives change the nouns and it's important to follow some rules like:
  • They are used before nouns: She is a smart girl.
  • They are never used in the plural: They are smart girls.
  • When you have more than I adjective you have to follow an order.
1 2 3 4 5
How Big How old What color Where from What's made of

3. What are you like?

(this question is used to describe the personality of the person or the appearance)
I'm talkative and kind.

4. What do you look like?

(this question is used do describe the appearance of the person)
I'm tall and young.

5. What do you like?

(this question is used to describe the preference of the person)
I like to watch TV.

6. COMPOSITION

Describe your whole family.

In negative: SUBJECT + DO + NOT (DON'T) + MAIN VERD + COMPLEMENT


I
YOU WORK IN SP.
WE DON'T WATCH TV EVERY NIGHT.
YOU STUDY ENGLISH AT WELBE PRIVATE CLASS.


And for HE / SHE / IT: SUBJECT + DOES + NO (DOESN'T) + MAIN VERB + COMPLEMENT


HE WORK IN SP.
SHE DOESN'T WATCH TV EVERY NIGHT.
IT STUDY ENGLISH AT WELBE PRIVATE CLASS.

NOTICE: Pay attentation to the verb when the auxiliary DOES is used, because the verb
needs to lose the S, ES or IES.
