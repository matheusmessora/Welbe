GRAMMAR - THE BASIC IS OVER

1. Review - Verb Tenses

Present Continuous or Present Progressive (action now).
 Subject + to be + main verb + ing


Examples: I am speaking with you.
Am I speaking with you?
I am not speaking with you.
Simple present (routines - habits)
I - you - we - you - they he - she it
Subject + main verb Subject + main verb + S (ES - IES)
Examples: I wake up early everyday.
You wake up early everyday.
He wakes up early everyday.

In Interrogative and Negative use the auxiliaries:

I - you - we - you - they Do and Don't
he - she - it Does and Doesn't
Examples: Do you wake up early everyday? Does he wake up early everyday?
I don't wake up early everyday. He doesn't wake up early everyday.


Planned Future (action in the future)
Subject + to be + going to + main verb+ complement

Examples: I am going to travel to Las Vegas next year.
I am going to buy a new house next year.
Are you going to watch that movie on Saturday?
What are you going to do next weekend?
He is not going to speak with her later.
He isn't going to travel with us.
What are you gonna do now? I'm gonna study(Informal English)
What are you gonna do next Saturday Night?(Informal English)
